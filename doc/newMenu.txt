The stages of new character generation are:
1) select gender and description.
2) select race appears next to gender, and press esc to go back.
3) select build appears next to race, and press esc to go back
4) select state getting mode appears next to all that and esc to go back.
5) It goes to a different screen with an overview and also lets you tweak the
   stats. you can still go back with esc.
6) then on the stats screen it lets you choose a name.
7) then there is a final overview with the name there on the stats screen.
